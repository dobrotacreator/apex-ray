# Code Of Conduct

Apex Ray contributors are expected to keep project spaces practical, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume review comments are about the change, not the person.
- Make technical claims concrete and verifiable.
- Respect maintainers' decisions on scope, timing, and release risk.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Deliberately disrupting issues, pull requests, or release work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or take other reasonable action to protect the project. Serious concerns should be reported privately through the repository's security or maintainer contact channel.
